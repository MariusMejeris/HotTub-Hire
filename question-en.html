"FAQ"

How long does it take to fill water in the hot tub?

This depends on how much water pressure you have. Hot tub, 1200 liters of water, takes about an hour to fill.

How long does it take to get the hot tub heated?

The hot tub, 1200 liters of water, normally takes 3.5 hours to get warm.

Do you have chlorine in the bath water?

We strongly recommend that you always chlorine the bath water. You are always expected to shower and scrub before bathing! This dramatically inhibits bacterial growth. Chlorine is of course included when renting our hot tubs.

Do you need to wash the hot tub after use?

The rental object must be returned in the condition it was at delivered. The landlord reserves the right to do laundry even for hygienic reasons.

When can the product be picked up and delivered?

One rental day is counted from 12.00 o'clock till 11.00 o'clock the following day. We always try to be of help if you have other wishes and it is feasible.

What is the best hot tub temperature?

•How long you want to bath.
•Whether it is a children to bath.
•Whether you are used to bathing or not.
It's more difficult to lower the temperature if it feels too hot, than to warm it up a bit. Therefore, start carefully and feel yourself! Guidelines for appropriate temperature: Pleasant: +37°C / +38°C. Very hot: +39°C / +40°C. Too hot: + 41°C

Are the rental products insured?

Yes, all products have full insurance. An insurance surcharge of 3% is calculated from the gross rental amount and is included in the entire sum. Detailed rental terms will be sent if rental conditions are created.

Is firewood included in the price?

Firewood can be purchased separately if desired.

I'm pregnant, can I bath?

We advise if you are pregnant and / or have heart problems from bathing in the hot tub or taking a sauna. Check with your doctor if you are unsure!

We want to share the payment on x-number of people, is it okay?

The deposit must be paid in full, but the remaining amount payable on collection can be split.
